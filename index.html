FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is PDFToolify really free to use?

Yes! PDFToolify is 100% free with no hidden charges, no premium plans, and no signup required. All tools are available to everyone without any limitations.

Are my files safe and secure?

Absolutely! All file processing happens directly in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. They stay on your device at all times, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What file formats are supported?

We support a wide range of formats including: JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, BMP (images), DOCX, DOC, TXT (Word), XLSX, XLS, CSV (Excel), and PDF files for reverse conversion.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes! PDFToolify is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Use any modern web browser to access our tools.
